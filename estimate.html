How It Works

Three ways in. One working bid out.

Pick whichever matches how you bid this project. You can mix them — voice some pieces, import others, edit manually.

1
Voice takeoff
Pair your phone as a mic via QR code — no app install. Dictate "Columns, fourteen W ten by sixty-eight, twenty feet six" and the app parses the section heading, quantity, shape, and length automatically.
2
KISS/BOM import
Already have a 3D model? Upload the KISS or BOM export from SDS/2 and your takeoff builds itself — pieces, connections, bolts, nuts, anchor rods, deck — all sorted into the correct sections.
3
Manual entry
Old school. Click Add Section, then Add Row, then click any description cell to search the 1,229-part catalog — W, HSS, L, C, MC, plate, bolts, nuts, anchors. Unit weights auto-fill.
Features

Everything you need to price a job.

One plan. Every feature. No seat fees, no upsells, no "contact sales for pricing on this module."

1,229-part catalog
Full W, HSS, L, C, MC sections plus plate, rebar, channels, bolts, nuts, anchors. Unit weights and unit type auto-fill on selection.
Buyout with pre-priced catalog
63 pre-priced stair/rail/ladder items — Risers, Landings, Handrails (2-line through 8-line), Pickett Rails, Wallrails, Ladders, Cage Ladders, Returns.
Live cost recap
Material (steel/tube/pipe/other/fasteners), labor at your hours-per-ton, profit markup. Bid price rounds to the nearest $100. Every line traces to a takeoff or buyout row.
Auto-assembled scope letter
Three-page PDF with your letterhead, logo, and bid summary. Includes, excludes, and qualifications auto-populate from your takeoff sections and standard lists.
Bid ↔ Scope view toggle
Hide every dollar amount before screen-sharing with the GC or client. One click flips the whole app to scope-safe, one click flips back.
Excel + PDF exports
.xlsx for takeoff and buyout. PDF for scope letter and internal cost recap. Downloads in seconds — hand off to the shop or your accountant.
Standard exclusions & qualifications
Set up your shop's standard list once in Settings. Every new bid auto-includes them. Still opt out per-project if a specific job needs different terms.
Furnished-only tagging
Mark sections as FO to exclude them from fab labor while still pricing material. The cost recap breaks out what you're fabricating vs. just supplying.
Every future update included
New features, catalog updates, improvements — every subscriber gets them immediately. No paid tiers, no premium modules behind a paywall.
