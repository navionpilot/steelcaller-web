Features

Everything theField Needs.

Designed with 30+ years of steel detailing experience. Every feature exists because an ironworker or superintendent actually needs it.

Hands-Free Voice Lookup
Say "Call 52B1" and the AI voice reads back the section, elevation, bolt specs, and any revision or camber warnings. Works while wearing gloves 60 feet in the air. Wake word always listening.
Revision Alerts
When you upload a revised model, SteelCaller automatically compares it to the previous version and flags every changed piece with a red REVISED banner showing exactly what changed. No guessing.
Full Bolt Specifications
Every connection end shows the exact bolt count, diameter, length, grade, and washer requirement — pulled from your KSS file and verified against your NC1 hole counts. Left end and right end shown separately.
Accurate Crane Pick Weight
Full assembly weight calculated from dimensions — main member plus every shop-welded plate, angle, clip, and stud. Not just the bare section weight. Calculated from your actual model data, not estimated.
English & Spanish
The entire app — every label, button, and AI voice readout — is available in English and Spanish. One tap to switch. Settings saved per phone so each crew member gets their preferred language every time.
Live Progress Tracking
The field crew marks pieces Complete as they go. The superintendent and GC see the erection sequence progress in real time — what's done, what's pending, what's been revised. No more radio calls asking for counts.
